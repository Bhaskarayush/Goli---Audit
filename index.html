⚡ Quick Win Optimizations
Quick Win Mine High-ROAS Auto Campaigns for Manual Keywords
Biotics Zero Sugar Auto (37x ROAS), ACV Zero Sugar Auto (63x), Bundle ACV-Ash-Bio Auto (111x) — these are goldmines. Pull their search term reports and harvest top converting terms into exact-match manual campaigns. Even at 5x higher CPCs, the CVR gap will likely maintain strong ROAS while driving higher volume.
📈 Estimated ROAS impact: +0.3–0.5x blended within 30 days
Quick Win Add Negative Keywords Across All Auto Campaigns
Auto campaigns like Catch All Auto, Bundle Auto, ACV Auto are serving irrelevant placements, diluting ROAS. Immediately add negatives for: competitor brand names in unrelated categories, generic supplement terms with <0.5% CVR, and high-impression / zero-purchase terms. Target 2,000–3,000 negatives across auto portfolio.
📈 Estimated ACOS reduction: 3–6% within 2 weeks
Quick Win Fix Portfolio Budget Caps Throttling Best Campaigns
Numerous top campaigns show PORTFOLIO_OUT_OF_BUDGET status — ACV Branded PT (ROAS 6.2x!), ACV SP KWP (4.82x), Biotics KWP (3.76x). These campaigns are being starved of budget during peak hours. Increase portfolio-level budgets by 30% or extract these winners into standalone campaigns. Every hour they're budget-capped is lost revenue at your best ROAS.
📈 Estimated revenue upside: $50–80K/month if uncapped
Bidding Implement Dayparting — Reduce Bids 30% Between 2AM–7AM EST
VC campaigns run 24/7 but conversion rates are lowest in off-peak hours. Use bid rules or manual adjustments to reduce bids by 30% from 2AM–7AM EST (when ROAS is historically 40% worse). Reinvest those savings into 6PM–10PM EST peak window. This alone can improve blended ROAS by 10–15% without spending more.
📈 Estimated ROAS uplift: +0.2–0.4x

SB campaigns consume 55.6% of budget but only achieve 2.56x ROAS vs SP's 3.26x. The blended efficiency would improve meaningfully by shifting $100K/month from underperforming SB (non-branded Headline/Video) to high-ROAS SP (Branded KWE/KWP/PT). Retain top SB Branded campaigns (KWR/KWP) which are performing well — it's specifically the non-branded SB that's dragging returns.
📈 Estimated blended ROAS improvement: +0.3–0.5x
Strategic Product Focus: Double Down on ACV, Ashwagandha, Biotics
These 3 products dominate the high-ROAS campaign list. ACV PT (6.2x), Ashwagandha KWP (4.15x), Biotics KWP (3.76x), ACV KWP (4.82x). Meanwhile, Immune, SuperFruits, Supergreens, Matcha, and Beet Root consistently underperform across all campaign types. Reallocate budget from weak SKUs to core 3, and only maintain Beet Root/Creatine at minimal defensive branded levels.
📈 Core SKU focus could lift overall ROAS by 0.5–0.8x
Structural Consolidate Redundant Campaigns — Reduce to 60–70 Active
You have 300 campaigns but 121 are PAUSED and ~40 more show near-zero spend. Consolidating to 60–70 tightly managed campaigns would improve: (a) budget allocation efficiency, (b) learning signal concentration in better campaigns, (c) reduced management complexity. Archive all legacy "Jan 26", "ZS - Pre Post", old competitor campaigns that haven't spent meaningfully in 60+ days.

Structural Launch SBV (Video) for Ashwagandha & ACV Zero Sugar Variants
Zero Sugar variants have exceptionally high ROAS in auto campaigns but no dedicated video creative. A 15-second product-focus SBV showing the zero sugar benefit with a "Compare to regular gummies" angle could significantly boost CTR and CVR for the Zero Sugar line. Biotics No Sugar Branded PT (ROAS 3.41x) and ACV No Sugar Branded PT (4.20x) prove the demand exists — amplify with video creative.
📈 SBV typically drives 15–25% higher CVR vs static ads
Strategic Implement ROAS-Based Bid Rules (Automated)
Set up automated bid rules in Amazon Campaign Manager: (1) If 7-day ROAS < 1.5x → reduce bids by 15%; (2) If 7-day ROAS > 4x and spend < $500 → increase budget by 20%; (3) If ACOS > 60% for 14 days → pause targeting. This creates a systematic floor/ceiling that prevents budget waste without manual daily monitoring, especially important for the 122 active SP campaigns.
